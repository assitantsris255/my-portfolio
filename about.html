Hey, I'm Dipak Bhandari

Hi, I’m Dipak Bhandari—born on 2065/03/12 BS in the picturesque village of Chhatrakot-1, Kachhaute, Gulmi. At 17 years old, I carry the pride and support of my family—my father Harilal, my mother Lili, three brothers and one sister—who’ve always encouraged me to dream big. My academic journey started at Shree Balkalyan Basic School (grades 1–8), where I built a strong foundation. I then discovered my passion for technology at Shree Saraswati Secondary School, specializing in computer engineering from grades 9–12. Today, I’m thrilled to take the next big step at Lumbini Engineering College, ready to dive deeper into the world of engineering and turn my ideas into reality.
